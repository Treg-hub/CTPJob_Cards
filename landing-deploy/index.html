Where your data is stored — and why Firebase is safe

All data is stored on Google Firebase, Google's enterprise application platform. It is not stored on a personal server, a laptop, or any device that could be lost or stolen.

South Africa Data Region

Data is stored in the africa-south1 (Johannesburg) cloud region. It does not leave South Africa.

Encrypted at Rest & in Transit

All data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption at rest. All traffic between the app and Firebase uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.

Google's Security Infrastructure

Firebase runs on Google Cloud, which holds ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, and other international security certifications. The same infrastructure used by enterprises worldwide.

Authentication by Firebase Auth

Login is handled by Firebase Authentication — Google's secure identity service. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Password resets are email-based.

Role-Restricted Access

What you can read or write in the app is enforced by Firestore security rules — your role determines your access level. Operators cannot see technician-only data and vice versa.

No Advertising or Analytics

Firebase is not used for advertising. No third-party ad or tracking SDKs. Data is used to run CTP factory operations modules you are allocated.

In plain terms: business data sits in a locked Google data centre in Johannesburg. Gert Peens holds Firebase Console admin access. Colleagues only see what their role allows inside the app or on the CTP Pulse board. Google protects infrastructure and does not sell your factory data for ads.

Permissions the app will request (6 total — all required)

The app guides you through these permissions during first-time setup. Each one enables a specific function. Denying any of them will cause the app to not work correctly.

1 · Location — "Allow All the Time"
Required for background geofencing — the app detects when you arrive or leave the site boundary and adjusts your on-site status automatically. You must select "Allow All the Time"; choosing "Only While Using the App" means geofencing stops the moment you put your phone in your pocket. Only on-site/off-site status is tracked — not your precise location.
2 · Notifications — "Allow"
All job card alerts reach you through push notifications. Without this, you will receive nothing — the system will escalate past you as if no one is available.
3 · Battery Optimisation — "Unrestricted"
Android automatically puts idle apps to sleep to conserve battery. If the app is "optimised," Android may put it to sleep while it is idle — this can cause missed notifications and prevent geofence entry and exit events from being detected. Setting it to Unrestricted keeps the app available to receive alerts at any time. Navigate to Settings → Apps → CTP Job Cards → Battery → Unrestricted.
4 · Display Over Other Apps
Priority 5 faults only. A P5 job card means production is standing — someone must respond immediately. This permission lets the app display a full-screen alarm over your lock screen and any open app so a P5 fault cannot go unnoticed. Without it, P5 alerts fall back to a standard banner that can be easily missed.
5 · Do Not Disturb Access
Allows P5 critical alarms to bypass DND mode — essential for night-shift coverage and for phones that are set to silent. Standard notifications are still silenced by DND; only P5 production-stopping faults override it.
6 · Schedule Exact Alarms
The P5 alarm uses Android's alarm system (the same system as your morning alarm) to guarantee the alert fires at precisely the right time, even if the phone is in deep sleep. Find this under Settings → Apps → Special App Access → Alarms & Reminders.

Data Use, Privacy & Employee Accountability Notice

Important notice for all CTP Gravure employees using the CTP Job Cards application.

1

Purpose of Data Collection

The CTP Job Cards app requests permissions such as location access, camera, and notifications to enable its core functions, including job verification, photo evidence, and accurate record-keeping. These permissions are required for the app to operate effectively in the field.

2

Data Handling & Confidentiality

All information collected through the app is used strictly for internal CTP Gravure business purposes.

We do not share your location data, job card inputs, or any other information collected through this app with external parties.

3

Primary Purpose: Training and Development

The main reason we review employee interaction with the app is to support training, coaching, and continuous improvement. Interaction data helps us identify where additional guidance or development may be beneficial.

4

Secondary Purpose: Accountability & System Integrity

As a secondary measure, we may review specific interactions to ensure the accuracy and integrity of records. This includes verifying what was completed and what was written in job cards.

The system maintains audit logs of all changes. This helps protect both the company and employees by creating a transparent record of activity and reducing the risk of unauthorized changes (such as one employee editing another employee's job card).

5

Review Process & Employee Notification

When the company needs to formally review an employee's specific interactions or inputs in the system, the employee will normally be notified.

This notification supports transparency and gives the employee the opportunity to provide context where needed. Our goal is to handle reviews in a fair and consistent manner.

Employees may also request to view the audit log of their own job card interactions and inputs.

6

Employee Responsibility

Every employee is responsible for the accuracy and honesty of the information they enter into the system. Intentional misuse of the app — including entering false information or making unauthorized changes to other employees' job cards — undermines the system and may be addressed in accordance with company policies.

7

Fairness & Protection

The audit trail exists to protect everyone. It allows us to verify what actually happened and helps prevent misuse of the system by any party. We are committed to using this information responsibly and fairly.

By continuing to use the CTP Job Cards application, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this notice.
