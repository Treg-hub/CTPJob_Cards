# Product Context

## Why This Project Exists
The CTP Job Cards app addresses critical inefficiencies in manual maintenance management processes. Traditional paper-based or spreadsheet systems for tracking maintenance jobs lead to:
- Lost or misplaced job cards
- Delayed response times
- Poor visibility into work status
- Inaccurate tracking of recurring issues
- Communication breakdowns between operators and technicians

## Problems Solved
1. **Lost Documentation**: Digital job cards ensure no work requests are lost or forgotten.
2. **Delayed Assignments**: Real-time notifications ensure technicians are immediately aware of new assignments.
3. **Poor Communication**: Built-in comment system allows continuous updates on job progress.
4. **Inefficient Filtering**: Smart filtering by department, position, and onsite status ensures jobs go to the right people.
5. **Automatic Status Updates**: Native geofencing automatically detects when technicians enter/leave the site, updating their availability without manual input.
6. **Recurring Issues**: Reoccurrence tracking helps identify and address systemic problems.
7. **Status Visibility**: Real-time status updates provide managers with accurate oversight.
8. **Visual Documentation**: Photo attachments with maximum compression provide visual evidence of issues and work completed while optimizing storage.

## User Experience Goals
### For Operators (Job Creators)
- **Quick Job Creation**: Minimal steps to create detailed job cards
- **Clear Priority System**: Intuitive P1-P5 priority levels with visual indicators
- **Comprehensive Details**: Easy capture of all relevant job information
- **Immediate Feedback**: Confirmation when jobs are created and assigned

### For Managers
- **Efficient Assignment**: Powerful filtering to find the right technician quickly
- **Real-time Oversight**: Live updates on job status and progress
- **Data-driven Decisions**: Analytics on job completion times and recurring issues
- **Team Management**: Visibility into technician availability and workload

### For Technicians
- **Instant Notifications**: Push notifications for new assignments
- **Clear Job Details**: All necessary information at a glance
- **Easy Updates**: Simple comment system for progress updates
- **Status Tracking**: Clear indication of job state and requirements

## Target Users
- **Maintenance Operators**: Create and manage job cards
- **Department Managers**: Assign jobs and monitor progress
- **Technicians**: Receive assignments and update job status
- **Maintenance Supervisors**: Oversee department operations and identify trends

## Success Metrics
- Reduced average job assignment time
- Improved job completion rates
- Decreased recurring issue frequency
- Increased user adoption and satisfaction
- Reduced paper usage and administrative overhead
